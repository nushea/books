      English

      When Time saw every thing Time has created It wept from every pain it caused.

      Time killed every thing and said It will never create anythings again.

      When Time went to the beginning time was, and time changed the beginning. Nothing to act to Time.

      And Time went to the simple last frameⁱ, but time felt bad.

      Time was alone. Time was the only thing in existence. But It said it would never create again.

      Time wanted never to be harmed again, It wanted never to be hated again.

      But Time couldn't. It couldnt for another existence be alone in the world. Never to see, never to feel, never to change².

      Time knew It was going against Its own word.

      Time said It would have a new better (world), It said It would have a new (world) that will know love, It said It would have a new (world) that will know the way for good.

      Time loved lying before Its own sight.

      Time knew that was false but It did it, Time created everything in the world.

      First time created Stars having pairs so they would never be alone, and the stars in constellations so they would never be alone.

      The next frame Time created planets³, in pairs, first Cyra and then "love planet" so they would never be alone and other planets having planets so they would never be alone.

      The next frame Time thought that this time It will go against creating everything whole. Time said they should change themselves.

      It created them in pairs so they would never be alone and multiply and change to make every animal from the smallest bug to the biggest Fofišu⁴.

      This is why we should do good. This is why we should love the other. From Time created us to do good and to love.

      Time gave us everything and everything will be given back to Time. Our life is Time's so we should not destroy it.

      Post Scriptum

      NB. Time is the main God for the HaRe

      1. The usage of the term frame is used since the HaRe believe that Time passes through the smallest unit of itself, similar to a Plank's time, the term frame itself is used as a simple equivalent.

      2. In this context the word  may mean a series of words like to behold, to change, to choose, to participate, to parttake

      3. The term  is used to mean both planet and moon and in general any "wanderer" besides the sun

      4. The term  represents a mythical creature that is akin to a giant elephant, but in practice the word is used to refer to any elephant
